What MyCarLife Actually Does

MyCarLife is a utility platform built specifically for the 3+ million Americans who live in their vehicles. Whether you're in a converted van, a sedan, an SUV, or an RV, this app helps you answer two questions every vehicle dweller asks:

"Will I get knocked on tonight?"

That's what the Stealth Score is for. It looks at your vehicle, your current location, and your behavior to tell you how well you blend in ‚Äî not whether you're invisible, but whether you look like you're supposed to be there.

"Am I actually okay?"

That's what the Dwell Score is for. Parking is just one piece of vehicle dwelling. Your overall stability depends on seven factors: vehicle health, legal foundation, resources, wellbeing, connection, purpose, and parking stability.
