Frequently Asked Questions

What is MyCarLife?

MyCarLife is the first all-in-one app designed specifically for vehicle dwellers. It helps you track your Dwell Score, find resources like restrooms and showers, connect with community, and thrive on the road.

Is MyCarLife free?

MyCarLife offers core features for free. Premium features are available through a sliding scale subscription ($1.99-$19.99/month) - pay what you can afford. We believe your tools should match your ambition, not your circumstances.

What is the Dwell Score?

The Dwell Score is a comprehensive assessment of your vehicle dwelling quality across 7 key pillars: Safety, Comfort, Resources, Community, Wellness, Mobility, and Sustainability. It helps you understand where you stand and track your progress over time.

How do I find resources near me?

Use the Resources tab to find restrooms, showers, laundry, water fill stations, WiFi spots, and safe parking locations. Enable location services for the best experience. All resources are curated specifically for vehicle dwellers.

Is my data private?

Yes. We take privacy seriously - especially for our community. Your location data stays on your device unless you explicitly share it. We never sell your personal information. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete my account?
