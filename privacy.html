Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 21, 2026

PZAI ("we", "our", or "us") operates the MyCarLife mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, username, and display name.
  • Profile Information: Vehicle type, dwelling duration, and other optional profile details you choose to share.
  • Content: Posts, comments, and other content you create within the community features.
  • Dwell Score Data: Your responses to the Dwell Score assessment are stored locally on your device and optionally synced to your account.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect location data to provide resource finding features. You can disable this in your device settings.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app experience.
  • Device Information: Basic device information for app compatibility and debugging.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's features
  • To personalize your experience
  • To enable community features and connections
  • To send important updates about the App
  • To improve and develop new features
  • To ensure safety and prevent abuse

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices:

  • Account data is stored on secure cloud servers (Firebase/Google Cloud)
  • Sensitive data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Local data on your device is protected by iOS security features

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data in these limited circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: Community posts and profile information you choose to make public
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate the App (hosting, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety

Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: You can request a copy of your data
  • Correction: You can update your information in the app
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and data
  • Location: You can disable location services in your device settings
  • Notifications: You can control notification preferences in the app

Children's Privacy

MyCarLife is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via email.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data:

  • Email: privacy@mycarlife.app
